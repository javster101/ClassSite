Our course offerings

For both the introductory and high school paths, we offer half-day and full-day courses. The half-day course consists of the learning portion of the day, which involves learning the key aspects of the day's concepts, and expanding that concept through exercises and examples.

The full-day course is the complete experience, allowing the first half's learning to be applied to exciting and insightful projects to solidify and gain insight into the importance of the lesson. In addition, students will have support from our expert teachers and TAs while working on their projects. This allows them to have ambitious and rewarding projects while not feeling overwhelmed.

Please contact us with any queries about lessons plans, course recommendations, etc.

Introduction to programming with game design

An excellent introduction to computer science concepts! Through making fun and exciting classic games, we teach students introductory programming concepts to kickstart their interest and develop a foundation in computer science. In this course, we use Python and PyGame to start their programming journey in an accessible yet powerful language, allowing them to keep growing their skills in the future.

This course is tailored to students in grades 5-8, accessible to those with no previous programming background.

Half Day (2 hours):

  • 10:00-10:30: Introducing a key programming topic for the day
    1. Variables, math, and basic control
    2. If statements, while statements
    3. For loops, simple functions
    4. Introductory PyGame
    5. Complex PyGame
  • 10:30-11:45: Practice topic through fun examples
  • 11:45-12:00: Play a review game to review and solidify the material we learned this week via Kahoot

Full Day (4 hours plus 1 hour lunch break):

  • 10:00-12:00: Same half-day schedule & lesson
  • 12:00-1:00: Break for lunch, optional fun group family-friendly games to relax
  • 1:00-1:15: Introduce a project based on the half-day class
  • 1:15-2:45: Work on the project, developing an understanding of the importance of the skills learned earlier
  • 2:45-3:00: Finish through fun optional showcases of the projects made this week, giving extra time to students that need it

Rates:
Half Day (10-12): $150/week
Full Day (10-12, 1-3): $240/week

High School/AP Computer Science


Planning on taking high school/AP computer science? This is the right path for you! We go through the critical parts of the College Board AP Computer Science curriculum and develop cornerstone knowledge in Java, the industry leading programming language. Through this process, we grow your student's understanding of programming, both practically and theoretically. We will use AP Exam practice material to create an authentic learning experience that matches high school CS education closely. We will also take diagnostic tests at the start and end of the week to help give a metric of growth for the student.

This course is applicable to students in 8-12th grade that are planning on taking high school computer science, especially those interested in the AP exam. Student with experience in Java will get independent attention to accelerate their knowledge through advanced problems & tips. Younger students with previous programming experience may join as well!

Half Day (2 hours):

  • 10:00-10:50: Introducing a Java concept
    1. Variables, types, IO, and conditional statements
    2. While loops, for loops, arrays, strings
    3. Functions/methods, lists
    4. Introductory object-oriented programming (classes)
    5. Recursive functions, code tracing problems
  • 10:50-11:45: Practice that concept through practice exams and live coding exercises/demonstrations
  • 11:45-12:00: Do a group review through Kahoot (quiz game) and answer any remaining questions

Full Day (4 hours plus 1 hour lunch break):

  • 10:00-12:00: Same half-day schedule & class
  • 12:00-1:00: Break for lunch, optional fun group family-friendly games to relax
  • 1:00-1:15: Introduce a project based on the half-day class
  • 1:15-2:45: Work on the project, developing an understanding of the importance of the skills learned earlier
  • 2:45-3:00: Finish with discussion of the days' material

Rates:
Half Day (10-12): $250/week
Full Day (10-12, 1-3): $350/week
